ODOP: "Where heritage meets innovation, and local dreams
find a global stage"

The "One District One Product" (ODOP) scheme is an Indian
government initiative aimed at promoting and developing traditional
industries and crafts in various districts across the country.
Under this scheme, each district identifies a unique product it
specializes in and works towards enhancing its production, marketing,
and export potential. The goal is to boost local economies,
generate employment, and preserve traditional craftsmanship.
Launched to revitalize rural and urban areas, ODOP plays a crucial role in
economic development and cultural preservation.

The One District One Product (ODOP) initiative in Jammu and Kashmir aims to promote indigenous crafts and industries unique to each district. This scheme empowers local artisans and entrepreneurs by refining production techniques and facilitating market access. It showcases the region's rich cultural heritage and fosters economic growth.
